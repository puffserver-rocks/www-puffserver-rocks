Welcome to PUFF: Check out our casual, movement and 10-man CS:GO servers.


How to connect to CS:GO community servers:


  • Right-click on CS2 in your Steam Library and select "Properties"
  • Under the Betas header of the Properties popup, click the Beta Participation dropdown and select "csgo_legacy - Legacy Version of CS:GO"
  • Close the Properties popup and wait for the old client to download
  • Launch CS from Steam. Select the csgo legacy version when prompted.

    • steam://connect links no longer works. You will need to type "connect casual.puffserver.rocks" in console

    • ABOUT


    We here in are proud to be an open community welcoming folks no matter who they are or which button they use as push-to-talk, all of whom get along swimmingly (provided nobody eats their popcorn with a spoon)! Of course, given all our different backgrounds, something you might say that seems innocent to you may not be so innocent to others, so if you're ever in doubt, we recommend you simply avoid the topic/comment altogether! Sometimes admins may give you a warning based on their interpretation of our community rules and expectations, and may work to quickly end a conversation you thought was okay. Their objective is to keep our community friendly, civil, and welcoming, so trust that that is their intention when they do step in, regardless of their personal perspective. Even if you felt everything was fine before they stepped in, we ask that you trust their judgement. Community spaces are intended to provide a venue for dialogue that fits the implied topics (i.e. don't dwell on your cat's litter box habits in #general!) in ways that meet our community rules and guidelines. If you see any activity that breaks these rules, let an admin know and they will address it if they are not already working to do so. If you're asked to move on or given a warning, please don't panic, but also don't publicly contest. Their objective is to end a problematic conversation trend, not to publicly call you out. Follow their guidance and there's nothing to worry about. If, however, you do violate our community rules despite warnings, you are at risk of being banned, so do bear this in mind. All that aside, your participation in makes it what it is, and we're so pleased you're here!
